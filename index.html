Designing a unified forecasting, capacity planning, and automated billing system to support IPO readiness across a 50-person customer success organization.

CRM dbt Snowflake Sigma BI NetSuite Billing Automation Audit & Compliance
02 / 07 The Problem

Three teams. Zero shared truth.

Customer Success
Managing $1M–$100M client forecasts in unversioned Google Sheets — no audit trail, anyone could edit at any time.
Operations
Capacity models running in Excel files that routinely crashed, blocking global resource deployment and delivery.
Finance
No reliable data for headcount planning or margin modeling — forecasts too unstable to act on.
Root cause: All three functions were tightly coupled operationally but had no shared data layer — changes in one team were invisible to the others until things broke.

05 / 07 Delivery

How it was built

Phase 1
CRM in 90 days
Stood up a CRM for the 50-person CS team, fully replacing Google Sheets with auditable, structured client and forecast records.
Phase 2
Data modeling & BI
Migrated all forecasting logic into dbt models in Snowflake. Built role-specific Sigma dashboards for CS, Ops, and Finance — including change history and root cause tracking.
Phase 3
Automated billing system
Built a contract and pricing source of truth to handle complex consumption-based agreements. Automated invoice creation directly into NetSuite.
Phase 4
Audit & IPO readiness
Designed a full approval workflow and field-level audit trail for billing data. Reviewed and approved by third-party auditors in preparation for public company standards.
